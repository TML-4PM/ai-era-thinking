## Operating principle

Books do not move from pod draft to publication without a human-in-the-loop design review. Pods can write, structure, promote and prepare assets. Troy signs off the visual identity, cover direction, launch look, and final release readiness.

## Release strategy

Cadence: one major book release per month from May to December 2026, with optional two-to-four-week acceleration where readiness is high.

Primary commercial model:

- free public excerpts to build audience;
- ebook / PDF edition at AUD 29-49;
- workbook / implementation pack at AUD 99-249;
- workshop / advisory package at AUD 2,500-15,000;
- enterprise licensing / training package from AUD 25,000+.

## Monthly release rhythm

| Week relative to launch | Activity |
|---|---|
| T-6 weeks | Confirm book scope, target market, buyer, channel and commercial ladder |
| T-5 weeks | Content completion sprint; pod assigns missing sections |
| T-4 weeks | Structural edit, chapter order, missing evidence and case studies |
| T-3 weeks | Design brief, cover concepts, visual system, social tile style |
| T-2 weeks | Troy HITL design review; asset freeze; metadata and registration checklist |
| T-1 week | Launch article, LinkedIn post sequence, email, PR/media, landing page QA |
| Launch week | Publish, promote, run live/session/webinar if applicable |
| T+1 week | Measure response, update product ladder, capture leads, create follow-on tasks |

## Workstreams required per book

1. Content pod: chapters, excerpts, final manuscript, source/evidence notes.
2. Editorial pod: structure, consistency, proofing, tone, Australian spelling.
3. Design pod: cover, hero image, social tiles, internal visual language.
4. Campaign pod: LinkedIn posts, email, PR, launch article, media/speaker pitch.
5. Product pod: price ladder, workbook, workshop, advisory offer, CTA.
6. Web pod: landing page, routes, SEO metadata, analytics, lead capture.
7. Ops pod: registration/ISBN checklist, release calendar, asset freeze, receipts.
8. Reality Ledger pod: status, proof, launch evidence, post-launch review.
