# AI-Era Thinking Book Suite Breakdown — Base, Growth, Chapters, Themes, Gaps

Status: PARTIAL WORKING MAP
Created: 2026-04-29
Purpose: give book pods a simple, usable map of every book: what it is, base material, growth direction, chapters/themes, gaps, and next work.

## Operating note

Master 4500 is not public. It is a private Supabase seed/source table, likely loaded from CSV/helper material, used to write and organise book material. Do not use the phrase publicly. Treat it as authoring input only.

## Simple model

- Base = what the book already has or can safely be built from now.
- Growth = what the book becomes after expansion, design, campaign and product wrap.
- Gap = what stops it being publishable.
- Pod task = what the writing/design/product pods should do next.

## 1. The Thinkers — PEOPLE

### What it is
A curated book about people: the minds, thinkers, builders, philosophers, scientists, strategists and operators who help readers think better in the AI era.

### Not this
Not Master 4500. Not a database dump. Not a list of 4,500 names.

### Base
- Supabase Master 4500 source material;
- expanded thinker profiles in repo/data;
- Neural Ennead framing;
- existing thinker/profile components;
- strong concept: who should I think with?

### Growth
A flagship public book and companion product: 50-100 curated thinkers, practical thinking teams, executive patterns, companion cards, workbook and workshop.

### Suggested structure
1. Why thinkers still matter in the AI era
2. The old minds and the new machines
3. Decision makers: judgement, bias and uncertainty
4. System builders: organisations, platforms and institutions
5. Power thinkers: governance, legitimacy and control
6. Creative thinkers: imagination, language and story
7. Scientific thinkers: complexity, biology and cognition
8. Technology thinkers: computation, networks and automation
9. Human-centred thinkers: ethics, care and dignity
10. Future thinkers: agents, BCI and augmentation
11. Building your thinking team
12. How to use thinkers without worshipping them

### Gaps
- curation rules missing;
- exact thinker list not locked;
- chapter order needs refinement;
- public tone must be human, not dataset-heavy;
- design style unknown;
- first 10-20 sample profiles needed.

### Pod tasks
- query Supabase Master 4500;
- select 80 candidate thinkers;
- rank into must-have / optional / appendix;
- draft chapter arc;
- produce 5 sample profiles;
- create design brief for PEOPLE -> Thinkers.
